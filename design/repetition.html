Repetition

Repetition is simply how we reuse the same elements on a page or design. It’s about patterns and connecting design elements together. In The Zillow Mobile App there is not a lot of repetition blaring out at you. However, if you look closely, there are several elements and text that when put together reiterates their message of finding the “home”.

In the center of the screen there is a search box with search examples; including, address, city and so on, Right about that is the message “We’ll help you find the place you love”. The word “place” is also used to describe your “home”. There is a photo of a house, and the main tagline “Reimagine Home.” All of these, allow the user a commonality in all the items on the page and know that this company is serious about helping you find your home.
