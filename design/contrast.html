Contrast

Company Name:

Zillow

How Used:

Contrast takes on how elements on the page differ from the next. It’s the difference between colors, light and dark, patterns, and rough or smooth. The principle of Contrast in web design is all about how colors contrast with other colors, and how dark contrasts with light. In the Zillow Mobile App the contrast is used within the typography. The light text against the darker background allows the text to stand out and allows the user to see the slogan “Reimagine Home” first. It is tricky to have font over images. There is often not enough contrast. Designers will tend to put a gradient, unknown to users, behind the text to help contrast the font with they image it’s resting on.
