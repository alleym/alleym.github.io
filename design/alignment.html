Alignment

Company Name:

Zillow

How Used:

Alignment is all about how one element on a website or design align or line-up with the other elements on the page. When things are aligned they present a well-defined site with a high attention to detail. The Zillow Mobile App creates center-alignment which often is not considered to be the best option. However, in this design it presents nicely, is balanced, and creates a nice starting place for anyone entering the site.
