Relax


Temple Inn & Suites is located in close proximity to many of the most visited temples of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. Known for our quiet, relaxing accomodations and beautiful settings; we are focused on bringing you that comfort of home.

While visiting the area and area temple, you will have the luxury of returning to your accomodations where you can rest, dine, and relax.
